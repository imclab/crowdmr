Peer-to-peer MapReduce

Crowd MapReduce enables you to run MapReduce without having to set up and maintain a set of servers. Instead, you ask people to volunteer their computing resources. Anyone can help with your job just by visiting a URL.

Crowd MapReduce runs on the client-side, with no servers involved. Communication happens using WebRTC.
